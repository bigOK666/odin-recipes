Fried Fish

This is the Odin's favorite food

It is delicious and can provide Odin enough protein.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Fish
  • Chilli
  • Soya Source
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix soya source with salt and Pepper
  2. Fry Chilli till it is soft
  3. Fry the fish together with chilli
  4. Put the mixed source in the pan
